DISCOVER OUR STORY

  • Koyla is an Indian restaurant located in the heart of Dunboyne.
  • It has been widely recognized as the pioneer of exquisite Indian cuisine in the Co. Meath.
  • Koyla restaurant has won numerous accolades for its food, service and decor.
